Holy Cross Convent Sr. Sec. School

Holy Cross Convent Sr. Secondary School is a minority school, managed by
the Sisters of Mercy of the Holy Cross, Hazaribagh (A registered charitable Society,
engaged in various kinds of educational and philanthropic activities through out India.)

Holy Cross Convent Sr. Sec. School, Ambikapur is an esteemed school and with a well-respected history of providing exceptional educational outcomes and opportunities for gifted and talented young boys and girls.
